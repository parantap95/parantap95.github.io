About Me














I turn coffee into code , use tabs over spaces and never broke production!

I like coding, especially for the feeling when you solve the problem, that sounds big to others, with your code (and it finally, actually works) and you feel as normal as any other programmer. I like to call myself a T-Shaped programmer wherein I possess sufficient knowledge about all the components of a full stack product development as well as specializing in Software Development. All of this while maintaining the same amount of interest in Data Analytics as well.

I believe that any product must be as intuitive and simple to use as it can be. I love designing and try to inculcate good design in what I do. I promote the ideology - ‘Code-As-Documentation’.
